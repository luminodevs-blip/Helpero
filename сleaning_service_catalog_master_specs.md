## 1. Core Operational Rules
1.  **Naming Limits:** All public names (Extras/Included items) are strictly limited to **18 characters** to ensure perfect rendering within the mobile app interface.
2.  **Pricing Strategy:** All pricing follows the professional psychological model (**.99** format).
3.  **Compare Pricing:** A \"Compare at\" price is mandatory for all add-ons to visually demonstrate value and discounts.
4.  **Duration Calculation:** Total order duration is calculated as: `Technical Base + Duration of all selected Add-ons`.
5.  **Base Scope:** The minimum order (\"Base\") always includes cleaning of **2 Living Rooms** (e.g., 1 Bedroom + 1 Living Room), **1 Bathroom**, plus the **Kitchen and Hallways**.
6.  **Service Boundaries:** If a task is not listed in the `Included` section, it must be treated as an Add-on or requires a change in service type.
7.  **Client Requirements:** Helpero is a \"Full-Cycle\" service. We **do not request** vacuums, detergents, or equipment from the client. The client is only responsible for:
    *   **Access:** Providing clear access to the premises.
    *   **Resources:** Availability of running water and functional electrical outlets.
    *   **Clearance:** Removal of personal items from surfaces to be cleaned (for Deep/Standard services).

### 1.1. Professional Terminology (Task Verbs)
To maintain premium branding and clarity, the following standardized verbs are used in all service lists:
*   **Wiping / Cleaning:** Surface-level dust and light soil removal.
*   **Scrubbing / Deep Scrub:** Intensive mechanical cleaning (for tiles, grout, floors).
*   **Vacuuming:** Dry extraction of dust and debris from floors and upholstery.
*   **Mopping:** Wet cleaning of hard floors with specialized detergents.
*   **Sanitizing:** Antibacterial treatment of high-touch zones (bathrooms, kitchens, handles).
*   **Degreasing:** Removal of heavy grease and carbon buildup (stoves, ovens, hoods).
*   **Descaling:** Removal of limescale and mineral deposits (faucets, showerheads).
*   **Neutralizing:** Chemical elimination of odor sources (not just masking).
*   **Extracting:** Deep fiber cleaning using industrial extractors (carpets, mattresses).
*   **Flushing:** High-pressure cleaning of drainage systems (AC drain lines).
*   **Polishing:** Final finishing for shine (mirrors, chrome, specialized floors).
